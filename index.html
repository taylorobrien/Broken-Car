Organ Harvester

  • High Concept: You are in a hospital trying to get as many organs as possible without setting the alarms off.
  • Player Experience Goals: The player should find the game easy, until they come to a point where the alarm is in their way. After the alarm goes off, the player will get anxious due to the music and shaking of the screen.
  • Genre: Killing Game
  • Game play: In this prototype of Organ Harvester, the player uses the arrows to move around. The player is to kill all of the people in the hospital, to collect organs, and get out without getting caught. There is an emergency door for the player to end the game upon entering.
  • Prototype goal:This prototype is evaluating states, tilemaps, effects, and using variables throughout states.
  • Features: The alarm effects.
  • Setting/Premise: The player is in a hospital.
  • Story: The player was paid to get organs, and where better to get them, than from a hospital? The player is to get as many organs as possible from the people in the hospital.
  • Target Audience: This game is intended for new-gamers, or gamers that enjoy simple game.
  • Play time: 1-3 minutes
  • Strategies: Avoid the alarms until you have collected almost all of the organs and can get out in time.
