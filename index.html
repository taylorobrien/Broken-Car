Broken-Car

  • High Concept: You are trying to survive in the woods, getting all of your tools to fix your car.
  • Player Experience Goals: The player should find the game easy, until they come to a point where they jump incorrectly causing them to die and need to start over again.
  • Genre: Escape Game
  • Game play: In this prototype of Broken Car, the player uses the arrows to move around, including the up arrow to jump. The player's objective is to get the items in the woods to fix their car, and get back to the car before dying.
  • Prototype goal:This prototype is evaluating states, large tilemaps, and using many objects that collide with the tilemap.
  • Features: The map.
  • Setting/Premise: The player is in the foods.
  • Story: The player was on their way to an event when their car broke down. They are stuck in the middle of no where and begin to search for items that will help fix their car. It is night time and the woods are not safe.
  • Target Audience: This game is intended for new-gamers, or gamers that enjoy simple game.
  • Play time: 1-5 minutes
  • Strategies: Avoid the spikes and wolves.
